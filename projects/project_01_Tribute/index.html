Linus Torvalds

Linus Torvalds is the creator and lead developer of the Linux kernel. The first Linux prototype was released to the public in 1991 followed by the version 1.0 in 1994. Linus currenlty works fulltime on improving Linux and is sponsored by the Linux Foundation.

  • 1991 First Linux kernel prototype released to public and open sourced.
  • 1994 Linux kernel version 1.0 released.
